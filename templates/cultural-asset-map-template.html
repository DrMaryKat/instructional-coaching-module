Cultural Asset Mapping Guide

Discovering and Leveraging Community Strengths in Education

What is Cultural Asset Mapping?

Cultural asset mapping is a strength-based approach to understanding and valuing the diverse cultural resources, knowledge, and practices that students and their families bring to the educational environment. This tool helps educators move beyond deficit-based thinking to recognize and build upon the rich cultural capital present in their school communities.

"Students' home and community practices should be viewed as resources for learning, not obstacles to overcome." - Luis Moll

Reflection Questions

  1. What cultural assets surprised you or challenged your assumptions?
  2. How can these assets address current learning challenges in your classroom?
  3. What barriers might prevent families from sharing their assets? How can you address them?
  4. How will you ensure that cultural assets are integrated authentically, not superficially?
  5. What support do you need to better leverage these cultural assets?
